Frequently Asked Questions

Should Electric Cars Be Charged Every Day?
There is one question that new and prospective owners of electric vehicles frequently have: should electric cars be charged every day? The answer to this issue varies depending on one's circumstances and choices. The answer to this one question, "Should I charge my EV every day?" actually just comes down to how often you travel and how much range your vehicle has. If your car has a 200-mile range and you average more than 100 miles daily, you'll want to plug it in and charge it daily.
When Should I Charge My Electric Car?
The first answer to this question is that due to the different battery types and sizes of electric vehicles, the charging times and durations are also different from each other. The brand, model, and battery type of your vehicle are important factors here; therefore, drivers usually take care to charge their vehicles by taking breaks, especially during long journeys. If you use your vehicle every day, it is essential to charge it during the day so that you do not encounter any problems during the journey. In short, we can say that a fully charged EV is quite necessary before the trip.
How to use the Saved Recipients feature?
Follow these steps to use Saved Recipients
1. Launch the Bluedot mobile app.
2.Log in to your Bluedot account.
3.Choose the Card Tab and navigate to the “Send Money” section.
4.Click on the “choose from saved recipients” button in the next step.
5.Select the recipient you want to send to.
6.Type the amount you want to send.
7.Verify the transaction details and select the “Send ACH” button.
8.Repeat the process to add more recipients as needed.
